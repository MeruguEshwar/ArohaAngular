ONE ON ONE (1:1)

Engage remote employees with
one on one meetings

Keka's One on One (1:1) meeting tool accelerates your
team’s growth by tracking each employee’s progress and
enables you to discuss the issues one on one. Team
managers or department heads can define agendas of one
on one meetings as it helps employees work on
improvement areas. The calendar of required people is
automatically blocked the moment you create a one-on-
one meeting on Keka.
